Lab Sessions

Introduction to Library Services

Perfect for the beginning of the new semster, this session introduces students to our full range of services including lending guides, print & e-resources, search hints and tips, referencing software overiew and subject guides. Students will learn everything they need to get started finding information for their courses

Information Literacy Skills

In these sessions, students will learn all about the different e-resources available to them. They will discover the differences between database, journals and repositories, as well as how to find literature reviews and other kinds of research resources. They will also learn how to evaluate information and use boolean logic to refine their searches

EndNote Referencing Software

These sessions introduce students to EndNote Web and Desktop, teaching them how to utilise referencing software and integrate into their research and assignment writing process
